Cash Stash rules

These rules keep every drop safe, clean, and fun across every city.

01

No trespassing

Do not enter restricted, closed, private, fenced, or unsafe areas.

02

No digging or damaging property

Do not dig, break, move landscaping, damage signs, damage buildings, or disturb property.

03

Be respectful

No pushing, blocking, fighting, harassing, or interfering with other participants.

04

Stay safe

Do not run into roads, climb dangerous objects, enter water, or take unnecessary risks.

05

Minors may participate

Minors are allowed, but parents or guardians should use judgment and supervise when needed.

06

Winner verification

The finder may be asked to send a photo or short video with the stash to confirm the win.

07

Respect sponsors

If a sponsor is involved, be respectful to their page, staff, location, and customers.
