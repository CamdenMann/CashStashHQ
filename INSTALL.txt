# Cash Stash Custom Chatbot Install

Upload these two files to the root of your website repo:

- chatbot.css
- chatbot.js

Then add these two lines to EACH page before the closing </body> tag:

<link rel="stylesheet" href="chatbot.css">
<script defer src="chatbot.js"></script>

Best pages to add it to:
- index.html
- cities.html
- city.html
- rules.html

## Important edits

Open chatbot.js and change:

sponsorFormUrl: "#partner"

to your real sponsor form section once your final domain is live, for example:

sponsorFormUrl: "https://cashstash.com/#partner"

## What it does

- Sponsor-first chatbot
- Participant FAQ
- Sponsor calculator
- Lead summary collection
- City routing
- Budget guidance
- Pushes serious leads to the sponsor form

## Notes

This is not a paid AI chatbot. It is a custom scripted closer that works for free on Cloudflare Pages.
